Bob's Burgers

Synopsis

Bob's Burgers is about a family of five who run a local burger restaurant. With burger specials served on a pun, the family works to keep the restaurant open while fighting off various self-caused crises.



Meet the Belchers

  • Bob Belcher - Dad, Head Chef
  • Linda Belcher - Mom, Musical Afficionado
  • Tina Belcher - Oldest Daughter, Boy Crazy
  • Gene Belcher - Son, Keyboard King
  • Louise Belcher - Youngest Daughter, Rabbit Ears


Other Characters

  • Teddy - Handyman and Neighbor
  • Jimmy Pesto - Bob's Nemesis
  • Jimmy Pesto, Jr. - Tina's Love
  • Mr. Frond - Well-meaning Counselor
  • Gayle - Linda's Sister and Cat Collector
  • Hugo - The Health Inspector
